THE RAT

Originally, there was a cat and a rat, who hated each other with a passion. These two were the worst swimmers in the animal kingdom, but they were both smart. They found out that the fastest way to the river is to hop on top of the ox. The generous ox agreed to carry them across the river. However, the rat was so eager to win that he pushed the cat into the water. The rat came first in the race.
