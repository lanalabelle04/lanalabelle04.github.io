Originally, there was a cat and a rat, who hated each other with a passion. These two were the worst swimmers in the animal kingdom, but they were both smart. They discovered that the fastest way to the river is to hop on top of the ox. The generous ox agreed to carry them across the river. However, the rat was so eager to win that he pushed the cat into the water; thus the cat never forgave the rat, and wasn't included in the race. Other variations of the story say that the rat just never told the cat about the race and did not even compete at all. Afterward, the ox and the rat made it to shore.

Quick-witted, resourceful, versatile, kind
